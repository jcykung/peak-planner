BC Backcountry Safety Protocols

If any group member becomes separated, disoriented, or injured:

  • STOP and stay put. Do not travel downhill off-trail or down creek gullies, as they often terminate in impassable cliffs.
  • Seek nearby shelter. Conserve body heat by sitting on your backpack to insulate from damp ground.
  • If you have cell reception, immediately dial 911 to activate North Shore Rescue.
  • Blow whistles in groups of three bursts (the universal emergency signal) to guide rescuers.
